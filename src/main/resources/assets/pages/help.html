Need help?

Click on the question that concerns you

  • How to register?

    • Registration

    Have access to all the features!

    • To register, you need to have a valid email address. This email will be used to confirm the registration.
    • Click on the button "Sign up" on the upper right of the page.
    • Fill in the registration form and click on "Create Account". The system will send a confirmation email to the given email address.
    • Open the confirmation email in your mailbox and click on the given link. This will activate your account.
    • Log in with your account and access to all the features of Book Trader!

  • How to buy a book?

    • Buy a book

    • Unless the seller left his/her contact information in the book post, the buyer needs to contact the seller using our Contact Seller feature (Note: you need to be logged in).
    • There are two ways to access the Contact Seller feature:
      On the list of all the book posts, click on the "Contact Seller" button of the corresponding book post.
      Or
      On detailed information page of a book post, click on the "Contact Seller" button.
    • Enter a message for the seller and click on "Send"
    • Wait for the seller to contact you.

  • How to find a book?

    • Find a book

    • To quickly find a book, use the Search feature on top of the main page.
    • Enter keywords or the price range of the book that you are looking for, and click on the "Search" button.
    • If there are books that satisfy your requirements, they will be displayed.
  • How to sell my book?

    • Make a book post

    • You need to be logged in.
    • Click on the "Post" button.
    • Enter information about your book and click on "Post" button.
    • Your book post will appear on the main page and we will contact you if a buyer sends you a message. Note that we do not disclose seller's email on our website. Your email will only be available to the buyer if you choose to respond to his/her message or if you enclose the information in the book post or in your profile page.

  • How to manage my posts?

    • Manage your book posts

    • To see all your book posts, go to your profile page by clicking on your name on the top-right of the main page.
    • Then, click on the "Click me to see the posts" button.
    • You will see all the book posts that you posted. You can delete a post by clicking on the "Delete" button next to the book title, or click on the book title to be directed to the page of the book post.

  • How to edit my post?

    • Edit a book post

    • To edit a book post, go to the description page of the book post
    • Click on the edit icon beside the book property that you wish to edit.
    • Enter the updated information and click on the "Save" button. Your post will be updated.

  • How to edit my post?

    • Add image to book post

    • To add image to your book post, go to the description page of the book post.
    • Click on "Edit" button.
    • Click on the image button.
    • Choose an image from your local files.
    • Click on the "Save" button and your image is included in the book post.

  • How to edit my profile?

    • Edit my profile

    Tip: Having a complete profile has shown to increase the selling rate.

    • To edit your profile, go to your profile page by clicking on your name on the top-right of the main page.
    • Click on the edit icon beside the field that you wish to edit.
    • Enter the updated information and click on the "Save" button. Your information will be updated.

  • How to rate a seller?

    • Rate a seller

    Rate a seller to promote trust and fairness during the book exchange.

    • To rate a seller, go to the seller's profile page. To do so, go the page of the book post; click on the name beside "Posted by".
    • In the "Rate me" section, select the rating that you wish to give to the seller and click on the "Submit" button.
    • Confirm your rating.
    • The seller's rating will be updated according to your input.

  • How to change my password?

    • Change Password

    • To change your password, go to your profile page by clicking on your name on the top-right of the main page.
    • In the "Edit Password" section, click on the "Change my password" button.
    • In the pop-up dialog, enter your old password, new password and re-enter your new password.
    • Click on the "change my password" button and your password will be changed if all the information are valid.

  • How to delete my account?

    • Delete account

    Note: We show our deepest regret for loosing you as our user.

    • To delete your account, go to your profile page by clicking on your name on the top-right of the main page.
    • In the "Account deletion" section, click on the "Delete my account" button.
    • Confirm your account deletion. Your account will be deleted from our database.

  • How to delete my account?

    • Go to social media

    • To go to your social media and possibly share the website with your connections, go on the description page of a book post.
    • Click on the social media icon that you would like to be redirected to.
